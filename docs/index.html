How PicklePro Works

Booking a pickleball court has never been easier. Follow these simple steps and be on the court in minutes.

🔍
Step 1
Search Courts
Select your city from the dropdown, pick a date and duration, then click Search Courts to see all available courts near you.
📍
Step 2
Pick a Court
Browse courts with details like pricing per hour, amenities (Indoor/Outdoor, Parking, Pro Shop), and location address.
👤
Step 3
Create Account
Sign up with your name, email and phone number. It takes less than 30 seconds and your details are saved securely.
📅
Step 4
Book Your Slot
Choose your preferred date, start time, and duration. See the total price instantly and confirm your booking in one click.
💳
Step 5
Pay Securely
Pay online using our secure Stripe-powered payment system. Your booking is confirmed instantly after payment.
🏓
Step 6
Play!
Show up and play! Your booking is saved in your account. Leave a review after your game to help other players.
